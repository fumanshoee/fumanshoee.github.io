On October 4, 2015, a video was posted of Parker Kane beatboxing into a McDonald’s cup which viewers have cleverly called, “McDubstep”. This video went internationally viral, and today, it has well over 600 million views on Facebook. With the success of this video, in March of 2016, Parker was invited to star in an official McDonald’s commercial, which was shown on all online social media and streaming platforms (YouTube & Hulu etc). Most recently, Parker was a presenter for TEDx and IZEA Fest. He was also featured in a wide variety of television shows including: The Steve Harvey Show, America’s Got Talent, Outrageous Acts of Science and Ridiculousness.

Hayley, Parker’s sister, has said that Parker is the epitome of a dream chaser who achieves more and more every year, and falls more in love with music every day.
